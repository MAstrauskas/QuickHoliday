Holidays

  • Barcelona, Spain

    Set in a remarkable location, 100 meters from the Mediterranean sea, The Hotel Barcelona features King size bedrooms with a sea view, terrace with sundecks and a panoramic high quality restaurant.

  • Druskininkai, Lithuania

    The ideal city break and health & wellness retreat. The Spa Druskininkai is an award wining hotel nested near the Nemunas river. This unique place offers salt water pools, full-service spa, river access and European cuisine restaurant.

    Rating: 4 stars

    Duration: 2 nights

    Pricing: €150-€400

  • Nice, France

    Located in the centre of Nice, The Hotel Nice is just a 5-minute walk from the Old Town. This boutique hotel offers a private pool, cosy double rooms and a traditional restaurant with a bar.

    Rating: 3 stars

    Duration: 3 nights

    Pricing: €300-€600
